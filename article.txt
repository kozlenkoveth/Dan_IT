Comprehensive Legal Support for Foreigners in Poland Since 2018

ONE PLUS is a fully registered legal firm providing a complete range of immigration and legal services for foreigners in Poland since 2018. We assist clients at every stage of integration into the Polish legal system — from applying for residence permits to obtaining citizenship, from business registration to resolving disputes in court.

Our founder, Tatiana Vyborna, holds degrees from the University of Economics in Wrocław and the University of Wrocław’s Faculty of Law, as well as postgraduate specialization in Legalization of Foreigners’ Stay. With over 10 years of experience liaising with Polish administrative offices (Urzędy), she continuously updates her expertise to reflect current legislation and serves clients in Polish, Ukrainian, Russian, English, and is currently studying Spanish.
